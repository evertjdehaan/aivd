# 46. Question time

# (a)
# Q: M, W, F, S, T, T, ?
# A: S, the letters are the first letters of the days of the week, two days apart
#      (it is a series of seven, and everything is about times/days/seasons)

# (b)
# Q: WR, SG, SR, ?
# A: FL, the letters are the first and last letters of the seasons
#      (it is a series of four, and everything is about times/days/seasons)

# (c)
# Q: ..., J, A, S, O, N, ?
# A: D, the letters are the first letters of the months
#      (it is a finite series, and everything is about times/days/seasons)

# (d)
# Q: If 7 + 8 = 3 and 10 + 9 = 7, what is 11 + 2?
# A: 1, the calculation is modulo 12
#      (and everything is about times/days/seasons and there are 12 months in a year)
